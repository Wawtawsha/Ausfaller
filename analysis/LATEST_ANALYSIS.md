## KEY INSIGHTS

1. **Tell stories, don't teach.** Storytime is 26x better than tutorials.

2. **Create curiosity, not relatability.** Open loops beat relatable pain by 16x.

3. **Go long or go home.** 60-120s beats 30-60s by 6x.

4. **Stop looping.** Non-loop content performs 3x better.

5. **Move the camera and yourself.** Dynamic face visibility beats static by 30x.

6. **High quality, not professional.** Over-production drops performance by 36x.

7. **Make people happy.** Joy-focused content beats trust-building by 22x.

8. **Be physical, not self-deprecating.** Physical humor beats self-deprecation by 6.4x.

9. **Reveal, don't compare.** Reveal narrative beats comparison by 143x.

10. **Vary your pace.** Varied pacing beats constant fast by 4.4x.

11. **Use original audio.** Original audio beats voiceover by 22x.

12. **Don't film in bars.** Outdoor beats bar by 8x for bar content.

## Methodology

- 1,009 total posts scraped from TikTok
- 987 posts analyzed by Gemini AI
- 873 posts with engagement data and posting dates
- Date range: June 2020 - January 2026
- Primary metric: Views per day since posting
- Formula: `views / days_since_posting`

This normalizes for the unfair advantage older content has in accumulating views.
